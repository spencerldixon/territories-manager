Territories Manager

Cloud software for effective management of in the field Sales Teams

What is the Territories Manager Software?

In 2016 we created an application that allow the users to manage territories for the sales teams of B2B campaigns in London. It not only allows organisations to quickly and efficiently identify territories they would like to send their sales team to but also records all the data of when a territory was last visited, who worked there last, how many businesses are there in the territory and how many of them are qualified businesses. Distance between the office and territory and time sales representative has to take to get there.

We have tested the Territory Application on Cydcor`s London office; GennGlobal run by Sarah Ward. The territory app was success in saving time and money as well as increasing reporting accuracy and data collection. On the shoulder of this success we would like to introduce the second version of the territory application for a wider audience. The improvements were made by adjusting all the features according to real user feedback. With the extra features we want to not only improve the experience for the organisations but also for the employees. We want to introduce further automation and save organisations more time and increase efficiency.

Create and manage your territories

Draw the boundaries of your territories on our interactive map and save them. The app will calculate various metadata about the territories and your staff will be able to record a particular visit against this territory as well as leave notes and mark potential clients within the territory.

Manage your staff

View in real time who is working which territory or dive deep into the history of visits for a particular staff member or territory. Staff can record data about their visits as well as add notes, documents and attachments to their visit.

Visits can be scheduled and staff will be assigned the best available territory (based on which territory needs attention next) and an automated confirmation email of their scheduled visit. Visits can be autorenewed allowing your system to run itself, always ensuring your staff are in the right place at the right time.

Improved analytics and automated reporting

Data reporting and analytics give employees and managers a report their number of approaches and conversations automatically calculated by the system.

Average hit rate and other metrics are available by the employee and territory to help identify which employee needs more attention and which territories yield below average results. Automated end of month PDF reports can be sent via email by the system.
